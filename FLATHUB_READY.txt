Q: Why do you need --socket=system-bus?
A: Required to communicate with libvirtd system service for VM management.
   Alternative (session libvirt) doesn't support KVM acceleration.

Q: Why --filesystem=/var/lib/libvirt:rw?
A: Standard libvirt path for VM disk images. QEMU requires read/write access.
   Similar to virt-manager's requirements.

Q: Why --share=network?
A: Only for user-initiated proxy connection testing (Test button).
   No background network activity. User explicitly tests proxy servers.

Q: Can you reduce permissions?
A: These are minimal permissions for virtualization management.
   Comparable to GNOME Boxes and virt-manager on Flathub.
